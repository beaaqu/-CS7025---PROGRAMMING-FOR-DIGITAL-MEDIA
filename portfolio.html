Canepardo Merch Illustration

Client: I Bellissimi di Zanzara Republic — a popular Italian YouTube channel known for its humor, satire, and cultural commentary.

I was commissioned to create a humorous, vibrant, and detailed vector illustration for the channel's official merchandise store on Fourthwall. The design centers on the Canepardo — a fictional hybrid between a dog and a leopard that became a viral online meme.

Using Adobe Illustrator, I focused on clean, bold line work and a vivid color palette to capture the hybrid's quirky appeal. The phrase “è un incrocio” (“it's a hybrid”) was incorporated using a playful, wavy type treatment to reflect the humor and tone of the project.

The final illustration was featured on t-shirts, sweatshirts, and hoodies in the channel's online shop, quickly becoming a fan favorite and one of the best-selling designs.

Key Takeaways:

  • Translating internet humor into engaging visual design
  • Balancing clarity and playfulness through line and color
  • Adapting artwork for multiple product formats and scales

FutureFunds Website Mockup

A responsive web design project exploring UX and visual consistency across devices.

This project focused on designing a responsive landing page for a fictional financial planning platform, FutureFunds, tailored to Millennial and Gen Z professionals. Both desktop and mobile versions were designed to prioritize clarity, usability, and a friendly tone.

Guided by UX principles such as cognitive load reduction, visual hierarchy, and consistent CTA placement, the interface promotes intuitive navigation and quick comprehension.

The color palette and visual language communicate approachability, while large touch targets, clear service summaries, and well-structured information architecture enhance usability across devices.

Purpose: To develop a clean, modern financial platform that feels both trustworthy and engaging.
Design Focus: User-centric flow, responsive scalability, and minimal distractions.

Highlights:

  • Unified design system across desktop and mobile
  • Integrated financial calculator and scheduling feature directly on the homepage
  • Strong hierarchy guiding users from information to action

Portraitry & Flora Fusion
Museum Catalog

Digital catalog design for an art exhibit by photographer Colette D.

Portraitry & Flora Fusion is a multi-page digital catalog created for the fictional Metromoheim Museum. The catalog balances detailed textual content with striking floral-themed portrait photography, using a modular grid to maintain rhythm, clarity, and flexibility across spreads.

My design approach emphasized contrast, balance, and hierarchy. A restrained monochromatic palette allowed the vivid artwork to stand out, while consistent spacing, alignment, and typography created a professional, gallery-like experience suited for digital reading.

Purpose: To showcase a curated photography collection in an elegant, readable digital format.
Design Principles: Symmetry and rhythm on the cover, asymmetry inside for visual flow, and strong typographic hierarchy to guide the viewer's eye.

Highlights:

  • Modular grid structure for adaptable, consistent layouts
  • Integration of high-density text and bold imagery
  • Minimalist palette that foregrounds the art's color and texture
